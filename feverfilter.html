The FeverFilter device is an innovative

Wall Mounted IoT temperature monitoring device

Mobile APP Maintenance

Background

The FeverFilter device is an innovative temperature screening device created by the Litens Health Inc., in response to the recent pandemic. FeverFilter helps to create a safer environment for businesses by performing an automated hands-free temperature screening for all incoming individuals.

Challenge

FeverFilter needed a simple and streamlined process to allow users to set up and customize each of their devices. Litens Health Inc. also needed an comprehensive IT infrastructure to support the large amounts of data that would be streaming to and from a large network of FeverFilter devices on a regular basis.

Solution

The OasisCode team worked with Litens Health Inc. to design and develop a mobile app for iOS and Android that communicates with the devices through a Bluetooth Low Energy connection in order to facilitate the setup of Feverfilter devices including connecting devices to WiFi and defining temperature threshold and notification settings as well as monitor the ongoing activity and status of the devices. Furthermore, OasisCode set up a robust and secure backend architecture to support the ever growing number of FeverFilter devices.
